HANNAH DOUGLAS

I am a woman with an attention to detail and effective project management skills. It has been apparent over the years in my studies at school. At a young age, I always knew that math and art would be my passions. However, I’ve always had to pick between the two which made my post-secondary choices difficult.
When it finally came down to which passion to pursue, I tried my hand at mathematics with a minor in computer science at Carleton University. Although, I was missing my artistic side far too much and took a leap of faith to change programs and schools. I wanted to incorporate my math and art skills into one subject of study. Thankfully, this decision was readily available to me.
I am currently at Algonquin College, working to familiarize myself with a number of different areas of interest. My favourite aspects of graphic design have been web development and illustration so far. It has taken many hard choices to get to where I am today, and I couldn’t be happier.
